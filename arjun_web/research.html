2025

Integrated Strategies for Building Resilient and Green Supply Chains with Graph Theory

Arjun Sudheer, Dr jerlin Seles S

Research Square / Pre Print

By applying graph theory to five years of logistics data, this research demonstrates that decentralized supply chain networks provide superior disaster resilience and environmental sustainability compared to centralized models. The study offers a data-driven roadmap for decision-makers to mitigate disruption risks while simultaneously meeting carbon-efficiency goals.
